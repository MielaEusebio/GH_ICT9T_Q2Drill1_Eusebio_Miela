The Types of Cryptocurrencies


Have you ever been curious of what the most popular ones are for your journey? Check it out below!



Bitcoin



It is one of the first ever localised cryptocurrencies! It was created during 2008 but was only officially used in 2009. Until today, it still holds up being one of the most popular types of cryptocurrencies in the whole world.


Ethereum



Similar to Bitcoin, it is decentralised. It was originally proposed in 2013, and was officially launched in 2015. It mostly specializes in NFTs (non-fungible tokens) as well as DeFi ( decentralized finance).


Tether



It was originally launced by Tether Limited Inc. in 2014. It is a stablecoin, meaning that it is pegged by the US dollar. It was designed like this to stabalize price steadiness.
